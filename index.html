Energy Tariff Pricing Analysis

Date generated: 2025-10-24

Overview

This project analyses a synthetic dataset of daily pricing data for an energy tariff. The goal is to estimate price elasticity and identify a price point that maximises revenue.

Price Elasticity Estimate

The estimated price elasticity is -1.45. A 1% increase in price leads to an approximate 1.45% decrease in demand.

Data

The dataset used in this analysis is available in daily_pricing_data.csv.

Reproducibility

A Jupyter Notebook (notebook.ipynb) is included to reproduce the analysis.
